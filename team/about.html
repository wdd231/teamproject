About Recipe Finder

Welcome to Recipe Finder, your go-to web application for discovering, saving, and managing delicious recipes! Whether you're looking for a quick breakfast, a hearty lunch, or a gourmet dinner, our platform makes it easy to find and store your favorite meals.

Our Mission

Our goal is to provide a user-friendly and accessible platform where users can explore recipes, add their own, and conveniently manage them. Cooking should be fun, easy, and accessible to everyone!

Features

  • Search and filter recipes by meal type.
  • Save recipes with ingredients, instructions, and cooking time.
  • Responsive design for desktop and mobile.
